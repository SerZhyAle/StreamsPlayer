#Built around the stream

The essentials for finding, keeping, and playing internet streams without turning the player into a dashboard.

01

One catalog, your choices

Filter by category, language, country, and media type. Search, sort, and pin the channels you return to.

02

Radio, video, and RTSP

Start audio in the main window and open live video or network-camera streams in a focused player window.

03

Refresh on your terms

Catalog updates are always explicit. STREAMS Player never begins a surprise background catalog download.

04

Local by design

Add your own stream, keep pins and playback marks locally, and use the app without an account or telemetry.
