StreamPlayer · Privacy

Private by default.

StreamPlayer has no account, advertising, analytics, telemetry, or service operated by the author.

Network access

The application connects when you explicitly refresh the public stream catalog, play a selected stream, or keep Grid mode active while visible video previews refresh. Those requests go to the relevant catalog or stream provider and are subject to that provider's policies.

Local data

StreamPlayer stores its catalog state, manual entries, pins, playback marks, and up to 64 cached preview frames under %LOCALAPPDATA%\StreamPlayer. This data stays on your device unless you choose to copy or back it up.
